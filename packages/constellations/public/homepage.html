How It Works

Constellations uses a bipartite graph structure to organize knowledge:

  • Atomic Nodes (circles): Fundamental entities like People, Ingredients, or Symptoms
  • Composite Nodes (cards): Collections like Events, Recipes, or Diseases
  • Connections: Only link atomics to their related composites

This prevents hallucinated connections and creates a logical, verifiable structure powered by live AI queries and enriched with Wikipedia and academic data.
